Stage 2: This is the moderate autism disorder where few medications for some years help people suffering from autism to cope up with the disease.

Risperidone: Helps to improve in the number of tantrums, aggressive episodes, and self-injurious behaviors

Aripiprazole: Helps in the treatment of irritability.

Clozapine: Treatment of schizophrenia and for reducing suicidal behavior in patients

Haloperidol: Helps to treat irritability, anger and uncooperativeness
