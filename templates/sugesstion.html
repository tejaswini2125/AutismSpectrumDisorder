Stage 1: This is initial stage and this can be treated by providing some therapies to the autistic patients.

Play Therapy: The goal of play therapy is to build social interaction and communication skills and, in the long run, to enhance ability to engage in novel activities and symbolic play.

Speech Therapy: There are aspects of speech and communication therapy that guardian can provide with relatively little training.

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA): This therapy is possible to do a quick online training and use ABA techniques at home through a program like making them do some basic activities like picking, throwing, etc.
