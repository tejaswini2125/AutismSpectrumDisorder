Stage 3: This is the most severe case of Autism. Patients with this severe Autism may suffer with this for their lifetime with greater difficulty than previous stages. Some of the medications may ease the difficulty, which patients have.

Sertraline: Helps to improve separation severe anxiety disorder

Oxytocin: Treatment associated with impaired social interaction and impaired communication, among other symptoms

Methylphenidate: Treatment for attention-deficit disorders and narcolepsy.

Venlafaxine: Treatment of major depressive disorder, generalized anxiety disorder, social anxiety disorder, and panic disorder.
